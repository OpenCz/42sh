# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **42sh**, pledge to make participation
in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background, skill level, or personal identity.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the team
- Showing empathy and patience toward other contributors

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Dismissing or belittling contributions without constructive explanation
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable
behavior and are expected to take appropriate action in response to any
instances of unacceptable behavior.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — including the
repository, issues, pull requests, code reviews, and any team communication
related to **42sh**.

## Enforcement

This is a closed academic project. Issues should be raised directly with the
team. All concerns will be handled privately and with discretion by the project
maintainers.
